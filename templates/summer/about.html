About this website

Why?

I own a wrist watch with world time feature. I don't often need to check the time in Tokyo, but when I do I like it to be accurate, not one hour off.

Cities that make use of Daylight Saving Time enable and disable DST at arbitrary dates of the year. Without an automated system to keep track of DST changes over the world, I always had some cities off.

I figured out that the best way to solve this problem was to receive a notification whenever a city enables or disables DST. I could not find any existing service doing that, so here we are.

Who?

This website is maintained by Nicolas Le Manchet. The code behind it is open source, you can find it on Github.

Help?

Do you want to add a city to the list? Did you find a bug? If you need anything just email hello@summertime.today.
